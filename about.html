About me

I discovered a passion for hiking and exploring the natural world as a child. I feel in love with the space, freedom and connection to nature instantly.
My journeys have taken me thousands of miles and allowed me to experience some of the most breathtaking sights and scenes that the Irish countrsyside has to offer.
This gallery is a collection of some of my favourite views and stunning beauty spots.
I hope you enjoy them as much as I do.
